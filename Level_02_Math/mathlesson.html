1. THE 5-YEAR-OLD LOGIC

Imagine you have a magic Star Wand. If you have 5 cookies and you tap them with the Star Wand * and the number 3, you suddenly have 15 cookies! In computer language, we don't use 'x' for math because 'x' is just a letter. We use the Star * to multiply!

2. THE NASA ENGINEER LOGIC

To leave Earth, a rocket needs Thrust. If 1 engine gives 500,000 pounds of push, and we have 4 engines, we tell the computer 500000 * 4. If you use a '+' by mistake, the rocket won't even wiggle!
