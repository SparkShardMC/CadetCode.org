1. THE 5-YEAR-OLD LOGIC

Imagine you have 10 space cookies and 2 friends. To be fair, everyone needs the same amount! The Forward Slash / is like a laser beam that cuts your pile of cookies into equal groups. 10 / 2 tells the computer to give everyone 5 cookies!

2. THE NASA ENGINEER LOGIC

On the International Space Station, we have a giant tank of Oxygen. If we have 100 units of air and 5 astronauts, we need to know exactly how much air each person can use. We tell the life-support computer: 100 / 5. If we get this math wrong, someone might run out of air!
